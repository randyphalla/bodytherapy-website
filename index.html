About This App

Body Therapy application is a therapy application. The objective was help people who need therapy, access Information about their therapy type, clinic, pictures, video. Communicate with their therapist or clinic from their phone. Help users to find therapy clinics based on the user selected.

Features

Search Therapy Types

Users are able to pick and search for their therapy treatment of choice. Get quick access information about your therapy type/treatment. Find therapies centres based on the choices you made, it will give you a list of clinics that will specialize that specific therapy in your area/location.

Message your therapist

You and your clinic are able to send messages to each other to schedule appointments, problem and question on your iPhone/Android.

Views pictures, videos, discussion and more

Users will have the opportunity to view pictures, video and discussions based on the therapy type choice. Users will able to gain access valuable information about based users picked. To get more information, go to the discussion page where users and therapist are able to read, view, edit, create, and comment on discussion.
